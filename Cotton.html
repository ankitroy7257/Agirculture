Suitable Soil for Cotton Cultivation

The loamy loam soil and black soil are considered good for cotton cultivation, the yield of cotton in such soil is very good. But in the present time, many varieties have come in the market, due to which cotton can now be easily grown even in hilly and sandy places, less water is required in the cultivation of cotton. Therefore it should be grown in well drained soil. For this the P.H. of the land. The value should be between 5.5 and 6.

Although the cultivation of cotton does not require any special climate, but when the plants start bearing fruit, then it is damaged by the frost falling in winter. It needs bright sunlight when it starts showing twigs.

There is no special requirement of temperature in cotton cultivation, when the cotton seeds start germinating in the field then it needs 20 degree temperature. After this, its plants require 25 to 30 degree temperature to grow. It can grow well even at higher temperatures.
