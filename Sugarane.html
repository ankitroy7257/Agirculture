SUGARCANE

Basic Information

Sugarcane is cultivated for cash crop. India ranks second in the world in sugarcane production. Apart from this, India is also second in terms of consumption. Apart from eating sugarcane, juice is also drunk. Jaggery, sugar and liquor etc. things are made from its juice. It is such a crop, which does not see any significant effect of climate change. Because of which it is also called a safe farming.

Here you have been given information related to suitable soil, climate and temperature for sugarcane cultivation, by getting information about which you can grow a good crop:-
Sugarcane can be cultivated in any type of fertile soil, but in deep loamy soil, its yield is obtained in greater quantity. For its cultivation, land with proper drainage is required. Because water logging increases the chances of crop failure. General P.H. Mann land is suitable for sugarcane cultivation.
Sugarcane plants require dry and humid climate. Its plants start giving yield in one to one and half year. Due to which it has to face adverse conditions, plants grow properly even in these conditions. Its crop requires normal rainfall, and only 75 to 120 cm of rain is sufficient. Sugarcane seeds require 20 degree temperature to germinate initially And when its plants are developing, then they need 21 to 27 degree temperature. Its plants can tolerate a maximum temperature of 35 degrees.
