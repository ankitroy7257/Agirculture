MUSTARD

Basic Information

Mustard farming is mainly done in all the regions of India. Mustard farming is a major crop of Haryana, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra. It is a major oilseed crop. Mustard Farming The special thing is that it can be grown in both irrigated and rainfed conditions. It is the third most important crop in the world after soybean and palm oil. Mustard seeds and its oil are mainly used in the kitchen.

Seed Specification

The right time for sowing mustard is September to October

Seed 4-5 cm Must be sown deep.

land selection
Mustard can be grown in sandy to heavy clay soils. But sandy loam soil is most suitable. This crop can tolerate mild alkalinity. But the soil should not be acidic.
