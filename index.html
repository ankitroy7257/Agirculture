WHEAT

Wheat is the main cereal crop in India. The total area under the crop is about 29.8 million hectares in the country. The production of wheat in the country has increased significantly from 75.81 million MT in 2006-07 to an all time record high of 94.88 million MT in 2011-12.

MAIZE

Maize is emerging as the third main crop in the world after wheat and paddy among the major food crops. the main reason for this is its productivity - because its production capacity is 25-100 per cent more than that of wheat and paddy. major producing districts/regions - chhindwara, betul, seoni
